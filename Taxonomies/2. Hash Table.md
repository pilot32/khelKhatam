> **Related Patterns**:
> - Two Pointers (offers space-time trade-offs for duplicate checks)
> - Sliding Window (pairs naturally with hash maps for frequency tracking)
> - Trie (alternative to hashed string signatures)
> - Arrays (in-place index hashing avoids extra structures)
