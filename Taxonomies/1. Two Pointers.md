> **Related Patterns**:
> - Binary Search (controls pointer movement by mid-point checks)
> - Sliding Window (a same-direction specialization)
> - Merge Sort (relies on pointer sweeps during merge steps)
> - Linked List Techniques (cycle detection, middle finding, etc.)
